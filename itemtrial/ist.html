About

Design Process

Ideation

The previous website had a few problems informed by the faculty and students. We started out by doing the heuristics evaluation of the website. We talked with the students and professors about the problems they were facing and thier expectations out of the website. Gathering all the data helped us figure out a few user-experience problems in the website.
