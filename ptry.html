Poster Design

Project

Information design and data visualisation have become very popular on the web in recent years. With so much information and data available to us now via the internet, info-design is a great way to make the virtual maze of facts and figures digestible. The three important factors in any information design are whether the information is Accurate, Accessible and Aesthically pleasing. Below is a sample work of statistical represenation of Google's Annual Revenue.

Work

  • Gathered all the data from various sources from the internet.
  • Segregated the relevant data to the poster
  • Selected the typography, color to be included into the design
  • Integrated non-typographic imagery that echoes and reiterates significant quantities, comparisons and fluctuations of data.
  • Gathered some feedback and re-iterated the design according to the needs.
