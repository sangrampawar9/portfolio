Icon Design

Project

Product icons are the visual expression of a brand’s products, services, and tools. Simple, bold, and friendly, they communicate the core idea and intent of a product.The project consists of designing icons for a website built by me in another course based on the sport of Swimming. The design process included research about the content, visualizing icons, rough sketches, reiterating and designing the final prototypes in Sketch.

Details

Skills- User-research, Visual Design

Type of Project- Individual

Tools- Sketch

MyRole- Research , Visual Design

Work

  • Rough Sketching of the individual icons relevant to each page of the website
  • Re-iterating the icons with added feedback from the professors and peers
  • Selected the typography, color to be included into the design
  • Integrated non-typographic imagery that echoes and reiterates significant quantities, comparisons and fluctuations of data.
  • Designed the final icons after a few iterations in the Sketch app.

Design

Icon-grid used

The icons are designed for a informative website based on Swimming in one of the courses in RIT. The content in the website are divided into 4 parts- Information about swimming, Learn Swimming, diet required for swimming, Contact. Website name- Swiminfo
